Project 8: Water Catchment Area Management app

In a team we created and deployed a water catchment area management web application. This web app is capable of not only showing the latest test site compliances but also historical ones as well. In addition, it shows time series data on various test parameters with future forecast on overall compliance based on the SANS 241-1 water quality standard. I was in charge of the Geospatial data page showing the waste water treatment plants (WWTP) and the various river water test sites on an interactive folium map.
